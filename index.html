I am a robotics researcher driven by the goal of creating intelligent systems that bridge the gap between human perception and machine efficiency. My journey into robotics began with a simple question: how can we make machines safer and more effective in handling labor-intensive, hazardous tasks while addressing their limitations in perception and adaptability? This curiosity has shaped my academic and professional pursuits.
