Mahesh Reddy Tapeti

I am a undergrad robotics researcher driven by the goal of creating intelligent systems that bridge the gap between human perception and machine efficiency. My journey into robotics began with a simple question: how can we make machines safer and more effective in handling labor-intensive, hazardous tasks while addressing their limitations in perception and adaptability? This curiosity has shaped my academic and professional pursuits.

With a strong interest in physics-based principles and learning-based approaches, my work at Robotics Research Center, IIIT Hyderabad, under the guidance of Prof. Madhava Krishna, focuses on developing visual systems to address contemporary robotic challenges.

I'm always eager to discuss new possibilities in the field. Feel free to connect with me on LinkedIn!
